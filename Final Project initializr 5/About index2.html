A one-of-a-kind collaboration to promote journalism and diversity among UT students.

The Daily Texan

The Daily Texan is the student newspaper of the University of Texas at Austin, serving the UT community since 1900. It is one of the largest college newspapers and one of the few remaning daily student publications in the United States.

Visit The Daily Texan

UT NAHJ

UT NAHJ is a student chapter of the National Association of Hispanic Journalists. UT's chapter was founded by students in 2016 to promote #MoreLatinosInNews and help students from all majors and backgrounds engage with journalism.
