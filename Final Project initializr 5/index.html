Negotiating Dreams

Over the last year, protections for undocumented students, known as DREAMers, have become leverage for negotiating border wall funding and immigration reform in U.S. politics. This is what these federal negations look like for the UT community. Through Negotiating Dreams, members of UT's National Association of Hispanic Journalists and The Daily Texan will share stories of DACA students at UT.
